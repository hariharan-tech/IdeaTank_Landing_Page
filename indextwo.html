IdeaTank is a webpage or web app where people from different sections like students , freelancer, investors can share their project idea or where people can solve a common problem or a professional can post some skill development courses.

IdeaTank Webpage is basically divided into Four Section which are:

For Idea-Thinkers :
to post their Innovative Ideas. For Investor / Corporates :
to invest in ideas for making a Startup. For Industrialist :
to get innovative solutions for their Industry based problems. For Professionals :
to upload course contents for skill development of students.
